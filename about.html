Who We Are

Kachi Kushwaha Samaj Hyderabad is a vibrant community dedicated to fostering knowledge sharing, helping each other, professional development, and meaningful connections among its members. It is registered society via certificate number TSGGDG 15374248 located at House no 11-5-234/2/E, H.P.Road, Moosapet, Kukatpally Mandal, Medchal Dist. PIN-500018

With a commitment to excellence and innovation, we create opportunities for learning, collaboration, and growth in a supportive environment.

Aim and Objects

  • To help poor students in their studies
  • To help the old age people by providing accommodation clothes and food
  • To conduct the social gathering for upholding culture and moral ethics in the community
  • To work for the welfare of handicapped poor and victimized person
  • To invest and collect profitable samples funds of the community as per the guidelines of the general body
  • To create interest among the people for taking active part in Kachi Kushwaha Samaj
  • To develop Kachi Kushwaha Samaj and facilities for the benefit of society members

Our Journey

2020 - Foundation

KMS Hyderabad was established with a vision to create a supportive ecosystem for professionals and learners.

2021 - Growth

Expanded our training programs and organized first community networking events with 500+ participants.

2022 - Expansion

Launched mentorship programs and established partnerships with leading organizations and industry experts.

2023 - Excellence

Achieved significant milestones with 2,000+ active members and transformed careers through our initiatives.

2024 - Innovation

Introduced digital platforms and expanded services to reach wider audience across multiple cities.

2026 - Future

Continuing to evolve and innovate to serve our community with cutting-edge programs and opportunities.
